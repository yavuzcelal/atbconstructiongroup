About Our Deck Design & Installation Service

At ATB Construction, we believe that a well-designed deck can completely transform an outdoor space—creating a perfect environment for relaxation, entertainment, and family gatherings. Our deck projects are tailored to fit each client’s vision, budget, and property layout.

We only use high-quality materials that withstand weather conditions and daily use. Whether you want a simple, understated platform deck or a multi-level structure with custom railings and lighting, our team has the expertise to bring your ideas to life. We pride ourselves on craftsmanship and attention to detail, ensuring every deck we build provides years of enjoyment and adds real value to your home.

We handle every stage of the process, from the initial design consultation to the final inspection. Communication and customer satisfaction are at the heart of our business—we work closely with you to ensure the finished product truly reflects your style and meets your needs.
