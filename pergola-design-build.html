About Our Pergola Design & Construction Service

There’s something magical about a pergola—it transforms a simple outdoor space into a destination. At ATB Construction, we design and build custom pergolas that add character, shade, and charm to your property, creating the perfect setting for relaxation and enjoyment.

What makes us different? It’s our focus on creativity and customization. We understand that a pergola is more than a structure; it’s a reflection of your personality. Whether you envision a modern, sleek design or a rustic, vine-covered haven, we bring your ideas to life with precision and care.

Our commitment to quality means we use only top-tier materials, ensuring your pergola is as strong as it is stunning. From treated wood to weather-resistant finishes, every element is chosen to provide lasting beauty and resilience. We also incorporate innovative features, such as adjustable slats for customizable shade and integrated lighting for evening ambiance.

At ATB Construction, we believe a pergola should do more than enhance your space—it should elevate your entire outdoor experience. Let us design a pergola that transforms your yard into a destination you’ll never want to leave.
